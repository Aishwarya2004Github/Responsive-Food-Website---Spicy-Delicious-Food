Special Selection

Delicious Menu

  • Paneer Paradise Salad

    Seasonal ₹80.00

    "Paneer Paradise Salad" is a vibrant fusion of crisp garden vegetables, succulent paneer cubes, tossed in a tangy dressing, offering a taste of culinary bliss reminiscent of exotic locales.

  • Savory Chicken Popsicles

    ₹300.00

    "Savory Chicken Popsicles" is a whimsical name for chicken lollipops, implying a delightful and flavorful treat reminiscent of the classic frozen dessert. It describes the dish as chicken pieces coated in savory spices, cooked to perfection, and served on convenient stick-like handles, making it both fun and delicious to eat.

  • Spice-infused Paneer Skewers

    ₹150.00

    Spice-infused Paneer Skewers" refers to a dish inspired by the flavors of paneer tikka. It involves marinating paneer cubes in a blend of spices, such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder, to infuse them with rich and aromatic flavors. These marinated paneer cubes are then threaded onto skewers and grilled or baked to perfection, resulting in tender and flavorful paneer skewers that are perfect for snacking or as part of a meal. The name emphasizes the infusion of spices into the paneer and the presentation on skewers for a unique and appetizing dish.

  • Royal Spice Symphony

    New ₹200.00

    "Royal Spice Symphony" describes a biryani dish that embodies the regal essence of traditional Indian cuisine. It features fragrant basmati rice layered with tender meat or vegetables, infused with a symphony of aromatic spices such as cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and saffron. Each bite is a harmonious blend of flavors, reminiscent of the opulent feasts enjoyed by royalty. The name captures the majestic quality of biryani, where spices come together in perfect harmony to create a culinary masterpiece fit for kings and queens.

  • Wok-Kissed Noodle Delight

    ₹125.00

    "Wok-Kissed Noodle Delight" describes a dish inspired by chow mein, a popular Chinese stir-fried noodle dish. The term "wok-kissed" emphasizes the traditional cooking method of using a wok, a versatile cooking vessel that imparts a unique flavor to the dish through high heat and rapid stir-frying. It suggests that the noodles are cooked to perfection in the wok, allowing them to absorb the flavors of the aromatic ingredients and sauces while retaining a satisfying texture. The term "delight" conveys the enjoyable experience of savoring this dish, which is known for its delicious blend of flavors and textures. Overall, "Wok-Kissed Noodle Delight" evokes imagery of expertly prepared noodles kissed by the wok's flames, resulting in a delightful culinary experience.

  • Mediterranean Sunset Pasta

    ₹149.00

    "Mediterranean Sunset Pasta" represents a pasta dish inspired by the flavors and ingredients commonly found in Mediterranean cuisine. It combines elements such as sun-drenched tomatoes, olives, capers, garlic, fresh herbs like basil and oregano, and perhaps a hint of tangy feta cheese or creamy mozzarella. The name evokes the imagery of a picturesque Mediterranean sunset, hinting at the warmth and vibrancy of the flavors within the dish. Just like a sunset paints the sky with a palette of rich colors, this pasta dish is bursting with a harmonious blend of savory and aromatic ingredients that create a truly memorable dining experience.
